Course Syllabus

Welcome to the Client (13-18 July)

Greetings and Introduction

Explanation of the Process

Task: Explain the keratin process in a voice recorder.
UNIT 2

Giving Information (20-25 July)

CLASS 03 Locked

Giving Instructions & Aftercare

Please don't move, wait a moment, sulfate-free shampoo.

Task: Give aftercare instructions to a partner.
CLASS 04 Locked

Prices and Service Duration

The price is $40. Pay by cash/card. Finish in 30 min.

Task: Simulate conversation about price & time.
UNIT 3

Customer Interaction (27 July - 1 August)

CLASS 05 Locked
